Notes & Swaps

  • Protein heavy-lifters: Greek yogurt + cottage cheese do the work without meat. Without one of them you'd drop ~25 g and need to add another chicken/tofu/fish serving.
  • Whey optional: This plan hits 180 g without a shake. Add one if you want to bump closer to 220 g (1 g/lb Attia target) on workout days.
  • Calories: 2,550 is a mild deficit for a 220-lb moderately active person. For maintenance add 1 tbsp EVOO + a second fruit serving. For a bigger cut, drop snack 3 to just cottage cheese (no banana).
  • Veg bulk: You're at 9 servings/day vs target 7 — the plan over-delivers on greens specifically. Can't really overshoot this one.
  • Variety swaps:
    • Oatmeal → quinoa or farro porridge with berries
    • Cottage cheese → skyr or plain Greek yogurt
    • Walnuts → almonds, pistachios, pumpkin seeds (rotate weekly)
    • Chickpeas → black beans, edamame, white beans
    • Chicken → turkey breast or extra-firm tofu (+1 legume bonus)
    • Avocado → 1 oz olives or an extra drizzle of EVOO
  • Alcohol: Not tracked in the score per your preference, but 0-to-2 drinks/week stays consistent with the longevity pattern.
